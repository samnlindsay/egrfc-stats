Data Notes

Data is sourced from multiple systems, each with limitations, and combined to provide the most complete view currently available.

  • Google Sheets: Primary source since 2020 (when I joined the club). For every 1st and 2nd XV game in that period it includes:
    • Date, competition, score, captain, and full team sheet
    • If Veo footage is available:
      • Scrums and lineouts won and lost for both teams
      • Individually coded attacking lineouts for deeper analysis
      • (From 2025/26) 22m entries for both teams and points/tries per 22m entry
    • From 2025/26:
      • MOTM and individual scorers
  • Pitchero: Individual scorers and MOTM before 2025/26, plus team sheets before 2020/21.
  • RFU (englandrugby.com): Team sheets and results across all teams in a league season for fair league-wide comparisons.
This does not include games before 2017.
This does not include games for the Vets or Colts.
If the team sheet or tries weren't logged on Pitchero (pre-2021), they're not included.

If you spot any gaps or errors, please let me know!
But remember, I do all this manually in my spare time - it's a labour of love, not an official club record.

Sam Lindsay-McCall
(Second Row, Lineout Coach, not a web developer)
